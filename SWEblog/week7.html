What's your pick-of-the-week or tip-of-the-week?

I've been really enjoying Atom lately. It may not have the most features compared to other IDE's, but it is more than enough for what I do and has a beautiful user interface. It also integrates with Git, which can be super useful at times. For instance, when I was working on last project, I had a merge conflict when I was pulling code. Atom showed me where the merge conflicts were and integrated it into the UI by displaying my version and the repository's version side by side. It then let me select which one I wanted to keep. Merge conflicts don't happen often, but I was glad Atom allowed me to resolve mine quickly.
